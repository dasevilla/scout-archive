# Golf Merit Badge
![Golf Merit Badge](images/golf-merit-badge.jpg)

## Overview



Golf is unique because the players police themselves. Other sports depend upon referees or umpires to apply penalties when there are infractions of the rules. In golf, every player is expected to act honorably, and the welfare and integrity of the game rely on every player’s honesty. This is why golf often is referred to as a “gentleman’s game.”

    - (b) Demonstrate that you understand the definitions of golf terms.

      **Resources:** [Basic Golf Terminology (video)](https://youtu.be/3qWh2Wruokg?si=8wh6SORdQITXot0t)
    - (c) Show that you understand the *Rules of Amateur Status.*

      **Resources:** [Rules of Amateur Status (video)](https://youtu.be/TeMdMFFOaTo?si=v2Fu90PKhD6_ixkf)

      - (c) Tell about the accomplishments of a top golfer of your choice.

        **Resources:** [LPGA Player Profiles (website)](https://www.lpga.com/athletes/directory), [PGA Player Profiles (website)](https://www.pgatour.com/players)

      - (b) Driver played from a tee

        **Resources:** [How to Hit a Driver from the Tee (video)](https://youtu.be/cia6EeHF7ZQ?si=ISZcQzXQSUXnpgo5), [How to Hit an Iron from the Tee (video)](https://youtu.be/JVsRCPjYTKQ?si=ErMm05iwjCb7DlMr)
      - (c) The fairway wood shot

        **Resources:** [How to Hit a Fairway Wood (video)](https://youtu.be/Fl-20OtZVoY?si=ReNADzqYp58hFvNT)
      - (d) The long iron shot

      - (e) The short iron shot

        **Resources:** [How to Hit a Short Iron Shot (video)](https://youtu.be/AAOoy8PPlsY?si=TTb4MX_e1u-zkB56)
